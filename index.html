About Me

Hi, I am Ameera! I am a Computer Science graduate (GPA 9.75/10, highest in my batch) based in Doha, Qatar, currently completing a Master's in Cybersecurity. I love sitting at the crossroads of digital marketing, frontend development, UI/UX design, and security, because I genuinely believe you should not have to choose between making something beautiful and making it safe.

I care about building digital products that work well, look intentional, and earn trust. Whether I am redesigning a website, launching a campaign, or tracking down a security vulnerability, I bring the same energy: curious, thorough, and always thinking about the person on the other side of the screen.

I have shipped live websites, contributed to AI platforms, built brand identities from scratch, and won a national award for software innovation. I am active in Qatar's tech community through QSTP, the World AI Summit, Cybersecurity Symposiums, and Katara innovation programmes, and I am always looking for the next interesting problem to solve.
